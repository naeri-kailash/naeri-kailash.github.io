Time and Habits

which timebox and habits techniques did you try?

Meditation has always been a part of my life as I come from a family that practises Buddhism which strongly encourages meditation as a daily practice. Although I do not always manage to maintain a routine of daily meditation, the concept of mindfulness is always very present for me. As of yet I have not tried any of the new techniques, however I find the idea of the pomodoro technique very interesting and would like to try it. I think this will be positive as I tend to immerse myself into study for long periods and then struggle to find the motivation to get back into it again that day.

what did you find interesting?

I found the idea of forcibly taking breaks even when you are in a 'flow' very interesting. It goes against how I study intuitively and I would like to see what kind of effect it has on my productivity.

what worked for you?

Meditation is pretty great. Otherwise I am yet to discover the other techniques!

which will you experiment further with?

Pomodoro technique.
