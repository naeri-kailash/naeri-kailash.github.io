I haven't attempted these exercises.

Try empathic listening - how did it go?

Empathetic listening is something I have aimed to maintain since I was quite young. Perhaps because I saw this skill missing in most people I wished to be able to provide this as I knew how meaningful it was. Feeling understood is very moving and healing and I have recieved a lot of positive feedback from people who have felt I have listened empathetically. Although it is a fantastic skill it can also be very draining. I used to do it a lot but over the last few years I came to realise I was giving too much in this was and started to also have boundaries to protect myself as well.

How did you find the loving kindness meditation?

This is a lovely meditation technique and definitely has a really positive effect on your own mental state in the long run as you are training yourself to observe and understand people in a different way from usual. Rather than judging people based on your own criteria you learn to try and understand people from a perspective that makes their story and actions make more sense.
