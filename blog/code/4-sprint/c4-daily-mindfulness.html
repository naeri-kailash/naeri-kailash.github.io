How did you find chapter 3 of SIY?

I found this chapter very interesting and helpful and he validated and articulated something I have experienced personally. After I started integrating mindfulness practice into my everyday life I started to notice many of the changes that he speaks of such as the ability to assess experiences more objectively. This has been a really amazing change in my life and I think it is fantastic that it is being taught in this way as I believe it would be life changing for many people.
